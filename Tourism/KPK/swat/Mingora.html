About Mingora:

ingora is a metropolitan city located in Swat, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Pakistan. Mingora hosts a population
of 331,091 (.33 million), according to the 2017 census, the city stands as the 3rd largest city in the
Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province and the 26th largest city in Pakistan.
In 1998 the population of Mingora was about 175,000. The city was once a major tourist destination and
was described by Queen Elizabeth as the "Switzerland" of the former British Empire.
