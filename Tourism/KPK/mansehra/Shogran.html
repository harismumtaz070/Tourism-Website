About Shogran:

Shogran is a hill station situated on a plateau in the Kaghan Valley, northern Pakistan at a height of 2,362 meters (7,749 ft)
above sea level. It is located 34 kilometers (21 mi) away from Balakot.

Shogran is located in the Mansehra district of KPK, amid the well-known and green-carpeted Kaghan valley, and is one of
Pakistan's most attractive hill stations and tourist destinations. Shogran is 65 kilometers from Mansehra city, 34 kilometers
from Balakot, and 222 kilometers from Islamabad. At a height of 2340 meters, it is a magnificent hill station with breathtaking
scenery and pleasant weather. In the summer, its moderate climate makes it the finest destination to get away from the blistering
heat of Pakistan's plains areas. It offers everything you might want in a dream vacation.
