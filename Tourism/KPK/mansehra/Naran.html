About Naran:

Naran and Kaghan are tourist destinations located in the Mansehra district of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
province of Pakistan. The phrase “Naran Kaghan” is actually a combination of two words, originally
derived from Naran Valley and Kaghan Valley that are adjacent to each other. Geographically, Naran
is a tourist town situated inside the greater Kaghan Valley at an altitude of 2,409 meters (7,904 ft).
It is located at a distance of approximately 23 kilometers (14.3 miles) further towards the north from
the mainland of Kaghan Valley. Kaghan itself is located 99 km (61.6 miles) from Mansehra city at an
elevation ranging between 650m (2,134 ft) and 4,170 m (13,690 ft).
